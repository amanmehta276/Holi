Holi Ka Itihas

Kyun manayi jati hai Holi?

1. Prahlad aur Holika

Holi ka sabse bada sandesh hai "Burai par Achai ki Jeet". Hiranyakashipu naam ka ek asur raja tha jo chahta tha ki sab uski puja karein. Lekin uska putra Prahlad Bhagwan Vishnu ka param bhakt tha.

Raja ne apni behen Holika (jise aag se na jalne ka vardan tha) se kaha ki wo Prahlad ko lekar aag mein baith jaye. Lekin chamatkar hua—Holika jal gayi aur bhakt Prahlad bach gaye.

2. Radha-Krishna ka Prem

Braj ki Holi Radha aur Krishna ke amar prem ki yaad dilati hai. Krishna ne hi gopiyon par rang dalne ki parampara shuru ki thi, jo aaj tak poore vishwa mein prachalit hai.

Holi sardi ke khatme aur Vasant (Spring) ritu ke swagat ka tyohar bhi hai. Yeh fasal katne ki khushi mein poore Bharat mein manaya jata hai.
